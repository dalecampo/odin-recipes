Firecracker Salmon

A glazed delight that bites back!

This dish has a peppered kick!

Ingredients

  • You guessed it: salmon
  • Olive oil
  • Sriracha
  • Paprika
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Hot sauce
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400ÂºF.
  2. Slice salmon into filets, skin on or off.
  3. In a bag, combine liquids and seasonings. Add salmon, close the bag, and shake.
  4. Heat a pan to medium high heat. Once hot, add oil and wait until shimmering.
  5. Cook filets for 4 minutes per side.
  6. Add salmon filets to oven proof dish if the pan isn't oven proof.
  7. Cover with any remaining glaze.
  8. Put oven proof dish in the oven for 9 minutes, then serve.
