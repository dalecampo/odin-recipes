Lemon Water

A refreshing cup of lemon water

The perfect answer to a summer day.

Ingredients

  • You guessed it: water
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Pour a glass of water
  2. Cut a lemon in half.
  3. Slice one half of the lemon and put those slices into the cup of water.
  4. Use a juicer to squeeze the other half of the lemon into the water.
  5. Kick back, relax, and enjoy!
