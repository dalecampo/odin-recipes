Potato Soup

A nice bowl of potato soup

This dish is best for a rainy day and will last you a full week.

Ingredients

  • You guessed it: potatoes
  • Milk
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Broth
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Dice an onion. Peel potatoes.
  2. Sautee onions on medium heat for 5-7 minutes until they soften.
  3. Throw in a few cloves of minced garlic and enough flour to coat onions, then cook for a minute.
  4. Pour in broth and milk. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Once potatoes are cooked, stir in sour cream, cheese, and seasoning.
  6. Turn off heat, then serve with green onions and cheese on top.
